Background

Dr. Mariappan Jawaharlal is the Associate Dean of the College of Engineering and Computer Science at California State University, Sacramento. He has more than 25 years of academic, industrial and entrepreneurial experience in engineering and is renowned for his innovative, engaging teaching pedagogy. As someone who is interested in stock trading Dr. Jawaharlal would like our team to create an application that can help to optimally analyze short term stock trades.

Scope

Scope Overview

Dr. Jawaharlal would like to create a smart and intelligent application to efficiently track stock trades. This data will be used to identify patterns which can help the client maximize profits through the analysis of brokerage data. The application is expected to be used for personal use initially, with the potential of commercialization in the future.

Which parts of the business problem will be addressed?

Dr. Jawaharlal is interested in the developement of a website to analyse stock trades. The application will consolidate all processes and tools into one workable application that will give the user the ability to help analyse historical data for increasing profitable trades over time.

What are the project goals?

  • An application capable of tracking trades and help identify stock trends
  • Track Stock Trades
    • Manual input of stocks or the option to modify (past) input
    • Overview (summary) of how a trader is doing each month
    • View a report of trades based on manual input or file input
    • Sort reports by various columns, parameters, or calculations
  • Pattern Identifcation
    • Perform calculations used to identify potential stock patterns
    • Create new strategies based on various criteria(s)
    • Add patterns to specific stock trades
    • Extra: Automatically determine if a trade fits currently defined criteria(s)
    • Extra: Automatically generate new patterns
  • Export reports to a CSV File
  • Import Files From Brokerages
    • Extra feature. Imput Files From Brokerage Websites Like TD Ameritrade
  • News Page
    • Extra feature. Not needed but may be useful to quickly access news related to market activity
  • All users will have individual accounts
    • To Begin, We Will have:
      • One Account For Client To Begin
      • One Account For Developement Team
      • Potential Commercialization with multiple users

Highlights
